Welcome back Emily.


Click on the Upcoming button to view upcoming appointments for this week. This will automatically display all of the upcoming appointments that are in the database.

Click on the Tutors button to add a new tutor or remove a tutor from the roster. This is just basic tutor information.

Click on the Schedule button to set or modify tutor hours. This is where you set each tutor's hours for the day of the week that they are working. First input the tutor's first and last name up top, then click on the day of the week that you would like to input time for. A start and end time box will display for that day, simply add the times (either by clicking in the box and using the arrow, or by clicking in the box and then just typing the time). The time format is Hour:Minute: AM/PM Then press submit to schedule a tutor. To reschedule and and change a tutor's time, simply follow the above process and add a new time for a specified day. The program will automatically remove the tutor's formerly scheduled time for that day and replace it with the updated information.

Click on the Reports button to get scheduling data and logistics. Currently when you click on this, a report will be generated that will include the students' major, year, if they speak english or not, appointment time, appointment duration, and appointment date. It will be a .csv file, which you can open with excel, that will save automatically to your downloads folder.

Click on the Disable button to disable the client side interface. Currently this is planned to work very simply, by deleting all scheduled tutor worked times from the table. Without tutor times the application cannot pull data from which to create appointments. But as soon as you re-enter at least 1 tutor's schedule into the database the system will be fully operational again. This can be easily changed to work a different way in the future.
